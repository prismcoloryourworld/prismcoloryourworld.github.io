The
Project

Are you ready to give color to your life?
Do you want more color in your life?

Prism is an application where you control a day in a person's life and the decisions you make could break the routine he/she is trapped in and bring colors into this person’s journey. Our goal with this project is to show people who are stuck in their routines, and people who think they do not have control over their lives that every decision matters. No matter how small a decision is, it has an impact on our lives, and we are, in fact, in control. The idea behind this project is to show that small choices in our daily routine that seem insignificant, can bring color into our world and improve life quality. With this, we want to encourage people who are stuck in a routine to make different choices in their daily life. People can improve their life quality, even if by a little, making life a little more colorful, joyful and captivating.

We have a solution to your colorless life

We present you the Prism App. We are aiming for the improvement of you life, helping you to understand where you can find more colors to your life.

We recommend

With a few simple changes in your routine I was able to develop healthier habits.

New opportunities

The notifications will help you to start choosing better in a already closed routine!
